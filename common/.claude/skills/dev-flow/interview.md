## The interview

Especially for medium and large tasks, or if smaller tasks are no clear, interview me relentlessly about every aspect of my prompt and goal until we reach a shared understanding.
Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
