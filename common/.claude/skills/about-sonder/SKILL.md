# About Sonder

A future project — an anonymous social storytelling platform. Not started yet, still in the idea/notes phase.

Domain: **sonder.house**. GitHub org: **sonderhaus**. Notes and early thinking live in `~/repos/nikbrunner/notes/01 - Projects/Sonder/`.

The core idea: people share personal stories anonymously, fostering meaningful human connection without the judgment and performance of identity-based social media.

The name comes from the concept of "sonder" — the realization that every passerby has a life as vivid and complex as your own.
