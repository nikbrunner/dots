Don't rely on Google tools for design or development workflows. Stitch was tried and abandoned due
to lack of control and Google dependency concerns.

**Why:** AI design tools like Stitch fight you when you want precision. Google has a track record
of killing products. Nik explicitly said "Fuck Google. Its never a good idea to rely on google."

**How to apply:** For design iteration, use the visual companion skill, Excalidraw, or Figma (non-Google).
For component development, build in-app with hot reload or use Storybook. Never suggest Stitch or
Google-dependent design tools.
